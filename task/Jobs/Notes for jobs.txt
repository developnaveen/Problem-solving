What is a Step? (Core idea)
A Step is a single, independent phase of a Job.

A Job does nothing by itself.
It executes Steps, one after another.

Simple one-line definitions
Job
A Job is the complete batch process from start to end.

Tasklet
A Tasklet is a step that performs one specific task.

Chunk
Chunk processing is a step that processes large data in pieces.
